**Gujrat**

Gujarat was one of the main central areas of the Indus Valley civilisation, which was spread across modern-day northern India and Pakistan.It contains ancient metropolitan cities from the Indus Valley such as Lothal, Dholavira and Gola Dhoro.The ancient city of Lothal was where India's first port was established.The ancient city of Dholavira is one of the largest and most prominent archaeological sites in India, belonging to the Indus Valley civilisation. discovered in Gujarat.
